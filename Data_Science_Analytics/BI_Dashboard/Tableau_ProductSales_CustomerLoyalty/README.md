Completed as part of the IBM Skills Network "Getting Started with Tableau Online" lab. This project demonstrates:

- Creation of interactive line charts for time-series analysis.
- Currency formatting and professional dashboard layout.
- Multi-sheet dashboard integration with KPIs.

## üéØ Business Context
Analyzed customer loyalty program data to visualize:
- **Product line performance** across 6 years (2015-2021).
- **Quantity sold trends** by product category.
- **Revenue metrics** for strategic decision-making.
- Identification of growth/decline patterns in:
  - Computers & Home Office.
  - Kitchen Appliances.
  - Smart Electronics.
  - TV/Video Gaming.
  - Photography.

## üîß Technical Implementation

### Data Preparation
- Uploaded `CustomerLoyaltyProgram.csv` to Tableau Online.
- Cleaned and structured product sales data.
- Established data relationships for analysis.

### Visualizations Created
1. **Product Line Performance by Year** (Line Chart)
   - Time-series analysis of 5 product categories.
   - Color-coded by product line.
   - Labeled data points for clarity.

2. **Total Quantity Sold** (KPI Card)
   - Formatted as currency (thousands).
   - Key metric display: $396.10 total.

3. **Total Revenue** (KPI Card)
   - Formatted as currency (thousands).
   - Formatted display: $222,215.70.
   - Primary financial metric.

### Dashboard Assembly
- Created "A - Product Sales" dashboard.
- Arranged visualizations for logical flow.
- Applied professional formatting and titles.
- Published to Tableau Online personal workspace.

## üìä Key Insights
- **Computers & Home Office** showed strongest growth (2015-2021)
- **Photography, Smart Electronics, Kitchen Appliances,** and **TV and Video Gaming** all declined from 2019, with TV and Video Gaming seeing the sharpest drop.
- Clear seasonal/annual patterns identifiable.
- Dashboard enables rapid business decision-making.
